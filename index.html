So, you're probably wondering

Who the heck is this guy?

If you've come all this way, it's probably a question worth answering! I'm a young game developer/narrative designer based in Antwerp, Belgium

But more than that, I'm someone passionate about digital storytelling. Everything around us is structured by the stories that are told. This adage is as true for the simplest act of making breakfast, as it is for the composition of a multi-tiered symphony.

Stories are where we go to get away, and where we feel most at home.

This is what connects all of my work, from my game design to the stories that I write; the websites I design and my work in 2D and 3D art. I am, at all times, experimenting with and exploring the what, why and how of digital storytelling.
