So, you're probably wondering

Who the heck is this guy?

Good question, and one that's always difficult to answer. To start off: I'm a British-born writer based in Brussels, Belgium.

But more than that, I'm someone passionate about stories. My belief is that everything around us is structured by the stories that are told. This adage is as true for the simplest act of making breakfast to the composition of a multi-tiered symphony.

Stories are where we go to get away, and where we feel most at home.

This is what connects all of my work, from my philosophical essays, to my game design, to the scripts, poems, texts and website copy that I write. I am, at all times, experimenting with and exploring the what, why and how of storytelling.
